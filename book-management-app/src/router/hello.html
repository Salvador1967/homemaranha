Honey: A Gift from Nature

Honey is a sweet liquid produced by bees from the nectar of flowers. Bees collect nectar, store it in their honeycombs, and transform it into honey through an enzymatic process.

Honey's Many Uses

  • As a natural sweetener
  • In cooking and baking
  • For some medicinal purposes (check with a doctor first)
  • As a beauty product (also check for allergies)
